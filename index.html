Vaagdevi college

Vaagdevi Engineering College was established in 2008 in affiliation with JNTUH, Hyderabad. It is approved by AICTE. Institute offers UG and PG education in technology and it also offers the PG course in management. The selection of candidates for these courses is made on the basis of the scores of the respective entrance exams. For the batch of 2021, the cutoff of the MTech exam TS-EAMCET was 69898 for the Computer Science Engineering branch.
